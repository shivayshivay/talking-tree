Mango Tree
Mangifera indica
Origin: India, South Asia
94%
The Mango tree is one of the most cultivated fruits in the tropics and subtropics. It can grow up to 40 meters tall and live for over 300 years. India produces 40% of the world's mangoes. The tree absorbs approximately 22 kg of CO₂ per year and provides shade, reducing urban heat island effect.
